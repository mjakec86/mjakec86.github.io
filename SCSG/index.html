Sandy Creek Sporting Grounds

With an exquisite topography surrounded by hills and forests, the Sandy Creek Sporting Grounds is the perfect scenic location for your next adventure. Woven into 100 acres of beautiful countryside, our pristine grounds feature an unmatched multifaceted selection of shooting challenges and sporting endeavors.

Justin Jones

Named for the beautiful stream that flows into Lake Oconee, Sandy Creek Sporting Grounds is under the design direction of international shooting champion Justin Jones of Great Britain, one of the world’s foremost experts in the development and management of elite resort shooting facilities. This luxurious sporting facility features a 20-station sporting clays course, a five-stand shooting range, a sporting clays instructional and games area, an air rifle range and an archery range. If you’re not a shooter, take advantage of an endless list of additional activities, including fishing, kayaking, canoeing and an off-roading course.

Off-Road Experience


Following the native contours and topography of the land, the off-road course presents a navigational challenge for novice to intermediate drivers in a controlled environment.

Sporting Clays


Winding through scenic ponds, rolling terrain, forested thickets, and a century-old pecan orchard, our sporting clays course presents guests with 20 signature location challenges. Often described as “golf with a shotgun,” sporting clays allow guests to enjoy a broad range of exciting clay movements, each providing distinctive entertainment for shooters of all levels.

5 Stand


Known as a compact version of a sporting clays course, the five-stand cabin is set above an ancient spring-fed pond and the surrounding lowlands. Providing guests an uncommon setting to shoot, the rustic but luxurious cabin also features a fireplace and a gathering area for post-shooting events.

Archery

Try your hand at one of the world’s oldest sports on our outdoor target range. Utilizing the latest equipment, our expert instructors will provide you with all the knowledge you need to hit the target every time.

Games

Nestled in a high grass meadow and offering a broad variety of challenging shots, the games and instruction course is the perfect area for team competition and lessons for all levels.
